Report an unsafe product

Trading standards are required to notify OPSS about any products which pose a serious danger to the public – in order to meet that requirement, there are a number of pieces of information we need:

  • Product

    Information to identify the product. This may include any or all of: category and type, make and model, unique IDs such as barcodes or serial numbers, web-pages where it may be viewed or purchased, any other descriptive information useful to identify the specific product (especially where the other information is not available).

  • Hazard

    Information that describes the danager posed to the public, including details of any incidents or events that may have occured.

  • Supply chain

    Information that describes businesses involved, and their roles in the supply chain, including registered business names, company numbers, address an contact details as available.

  • Corrective action

    Information that describes corrective actions taken (by you or any business), including details of date, legistaltion and documentary evidence where applicable.

  • Suporting images and attachments

    Files that suppliment the report, including any or all of: test results, risk assessments, product images, images showing damage or injury caused, receipts of purchase, or any other relevant file

  • Your system reference

    The case number or equivalent on your system, that will aid in cross-referencing, should the need arise.
